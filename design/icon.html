Recommendation Open Halo (Concept 5)

The Open Halo is the only mark that borrows its geometry directly from the product rather than from wand iconography in general — its ring is left open at the top exactly the way the real Halo is (border-top:none tracing only the notch's sides + bottom, per showcase.html). The wand casts light up through that same gap. It reads clean at 16px because it's two strokes and a glow — no fine detail to lose — and it ties Halo and Bloom together in one glyph instead of picking just one.

Wand + Halo Ring (Concept 2) is the strong runner-up if a fully-closed, more classic "halo" reads better in user testing — it's more universally legible as "a ring" at a glance, just less literally *our* Halo shape.
