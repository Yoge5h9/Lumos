Non-notch fallbacks

Recommended default

Menu-bar LED only

Look for: nothing above the bar — only the small dot.

No screen glow at all — just the colored dot already living in the menu bar. Works identically on every Mac, notch or not, never looks like a rendering artifact, and stays out of the way. This is what ships by default on non-notch Macs.

Sat 1:24
Optional secondary

Top-center glow bar (faux-notch)

Look for: a thin, soft light line centered at the very top — not a shape.

A slim rounded line of light with a soft bloom beneath it, centered where a notch would sit. Deliberately understated — a hint, not a shout — so it never reads as a fake notch or a graphical glitch. Available for users who want a touch more glanceability than the LED alone.

Also considered, rejected: a full-width top-edge glow (competed with bright wallpapers) and a floating ambient percentage pill (too much persistent chrome).
Both finalists are non-notch fallbacks for the Halo — on notch-equipped Macs, Lumos always prefers the real Halo + Bloom around the physical notch.
